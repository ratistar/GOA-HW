My Favorite YouTubers

MrBeast

MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson) is known for his viral challenges, philanthropy, and large-scale stunts. He creates videos that often involve massive amounts of money, and he is one of the most popular YouTubers worldwide.

Markiplier

Markiplier (Mark Fischbach) is a YouTuber famous for his comedic gameplay videos, especially in horror games. He has a massive following and is well-known for his energetic and funny commentary.

Casey Neistat

Casey Neistat is a filmmaker, vlogger, and entrepreneur. He is known for his high-energy vlogs, creative filmmaking style, and inspirational content about travel, life, and entrepreneurship.
